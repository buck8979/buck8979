Terms & Conditions:

1. By submitting a Booking Registration Form to the 40th Reunion Committee, you  confirm that you consent to these terms and conditions.

 

2. Only people who are eligible to be classmates of the Lewis & Harris 40th Reunion 2018 can attend the Friday evening event (12th April 2019). The Saturday event is open to partners and other events are open to all.

 

3. Your booking is only confirmed when a Booking Registration Form and full payment has been received. We will contact you to confirm this by e-mail or by post ahead of the event.

 

4. Tickets are limited by venue size and will be allocated on a first-come, first-served basis once both registration and payment are confirmed.

Data Protection Act 1998

 

We will not share your personal information with any third party.

 

Apart from your name and e-mail address, all other data submitted to us as part of the booking process will be securely deleted after the event has taken place.

 

We will retain your name and e-mail address to allow us to correspond with you about related news for a period of six months after the event. Please contact us if you do not wish us to do so and we will remove all your data from our records.
