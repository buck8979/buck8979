Junior Secondaries

The closing of Lionel Secondary School in the summer of 2015 marked the end of an era for schools in Lewis and Harris. It was the last surviving junior secondary school and, in many people's eyes, this and the other schools below were a sad loss of what was an excellent provision in our rural communities. All secondary-age children in Lewis and Harris are taught in two modern new-build schools, The Nicolson Institute in Stornoway and Sir E Scott in Tarbert.
